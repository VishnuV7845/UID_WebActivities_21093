The Blackout

The Blackout is a Capture the Flag event. CTFs are a special kind of information security competitions. There are three commom types: Jeopardy, Attack-Defense, and mixed. The Blackout is aJeopardy style CTF.

Jeopardy-style CTFs has several challenges or tasks in a range of categoriesasuch as Web Exploitation, Forensic, Crypto, Binar, etc. Teams gain some points for every solved task. More points are awarded for more complicated tasks. The next task in the chain can be opened only after some of the teams solve the previous tasks. Then the game time is over, the sum of points shows you the CTF winner.

Plot

A mysterious ship has arrived on Earth's horizon. Humans have tried to make contact, but the "Others," as the humans have taken to calling them now, has rejected all of the tries. However, now the "Others" pose a significant threat to us, because they have just launched a globe-wide cyber attack on military establishments. Will you help us recover our files and break the encryption to gain access to the encrypted files, soldier?

Tasks will be revealed aftert the competition starts!
