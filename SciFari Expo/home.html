About Scifari 2022

This is Scifari 2022.

We are an organization in Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham that organizes a Science Expo every year, and we have been doing so for 5 years now. Students from all across the globe meet here to showcase their newest inventions, and have discussions with other like minded people. Besides that, we also host a number of events and competitions for the students to participate in and win prizes.

This Expo is also a place for students to find mentors under whom they could undertake their research work. Furthermore, the college also provides them support to undertake their research work.
